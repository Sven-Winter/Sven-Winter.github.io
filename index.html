Dr. rer. nat. Sven Winter is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Research Institute of Wildlife Ecology, University of Veterinary Medicine Vienna. He holds a doctorate from the Goethe-University Frankfurt am Main, and is a member of the IUCN SSC Giraffe and Okapi Specialist Group. Dr. Winter is also an academic editor for PlosONE.
