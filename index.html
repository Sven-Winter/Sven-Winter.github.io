About Me

Dr. rer. nat. Sven Winter is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Research Institute of Wildlife Ecology, University of Veterinary Medicine Vienna. His institutional e-mail is sven.winter@vetmeduni.ac.at, and his independent e-mail is svenwinter.research@gmail.com. He received his doctorate from the Goethe-University Frankfurt am Main and is a member of the IUCN SSC Giraffe and Okapi Specialist Group. Dr. Sven Winter is also an academic editor for PlosONE.
