Dr. rer. nat. Sven Winter is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Research Institute of Wildlife Ecology, University of Veterinary Medicine Vienna. He holds a doctorate from the Goethe-University Frankfurt am Main and is a member of the IUCN SSC Giraffe and Okapi Specialist Group. He is also an Academic Editor for PlosONE. Dr. Winter can be contacted at his institutional email sven.winter@vetmeduni.ac.at or his independent email svenwinter.research@gmail.com.
