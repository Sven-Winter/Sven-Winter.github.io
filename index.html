About Me

Dr. rer. nat. Sven Winter is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Research Institute of Wildlife Ecology, University of Veterinary Medicine Vienna. He received his doctorate from the Goethe-University Frankfurt am Main and is a member of the IUCN SSC Giraffe and Okapi Specialist Group. Dr. Winter is also an Academic Editor for PlosONE.
